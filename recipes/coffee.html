Instant Coffee Recipe

If you would like a quick and delicious Coffee. Then this recipe is just for you!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Milk
  • Ice
  • Caramel Syrup
  • 1 Tbsp of Vanilla Syrup
  • A Tsp of Instant Coffee
  • Milk Frother
  • 4oz of Hot Water

If you are in need of of a milk frother, you may purchase a frother from Amazon

Steps to make Coffee

  1. Pour Caramel Syrup inside of a glass.
  2. Add half a Cup of Ice in the glass.
  3. Add 1 Tbsp of Vanilla Syrup.
  4. Add 1 Cup of Milk
  5. In another cup, add 4oz of Hot Water and mix in a Tsp of Instant Coffee.
  6. Froth the Coffee for a couple of seconds.
  7. Then add the Coffee to the glass of Milk.
  8. Finally, mix your drink with your Straw and Enjoy!
