Tacos Recipe

Would you like to prepare tacos at the comfort of your own home without cooking the meal from scratch? You can have tacos cooked within 15 minutes after gathering a few ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 3 Lbs of marinated Pastor (for 2-3 people)
  • Corn Tortillas
  • Cilantro
  • Cucumber slices
  • Lime
  • White Onion
  • Salsa of your choice

Note: The Pastor can be bought prepared in your closest mexican market. While you are already there you can buy any ingredients you're missing, including Salsa already prepared!

Steps to Cook Tacos

  1. Start heating up the tortillas in the Comal, be sure to keep an eye on the tortillas and continue swapping tortillas as needed.
  2. Heat up the Pan, once its hot you can add the pastor in the pan
  3. As both are cooking you can start dicing the white onion and place in a separate bowl on one side.
  4. Then peel and slice the cucumber.
  5. Cut the Lime into wedges.
  6. Stem the Cilantro and place on the other side of the bowl with the onion.
  7. Once you have the pastor is cooked you can turn off the stove and start plating the tortillas
  8. Add the Pastor, cilantro, onion and cucumber in the tortillas
  9. Finally add the Salsa of your choice and squeeze some lime in your Taco.
  10. Your Tacos are now ready. Enjoy your first bite!
